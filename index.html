Introducing Polystox App

India's leading online marketplace for the polymer industry. Polystox App makes transactions easy, transforming how buyers and sellers connect and do business worldwide. With real-time updates and guaranteed best rates, it's your one-stop solution. No more contacting multiple buyers and sellers separately. Explore a wide range of materials, all at your fingertips. Welcome to the future of polymer trading with Polystox App!

Offer & Demand

Polystox enables sellers nationwide to list materials at their preferred prices, locations, and quantities, while buyers can purchase directly or create custom demands.

Foreign Market

This feature allows users to directly procure materials with international suppliers and indentors, securing bulk deal prices with ease.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to revolutionize the polymer industry by building a connected, transparent, and efficient global marketplace where every stakeholder can benefit from seamless transactions, real-time updates, and the best rates.

Our mission is to create a platform that simplifies the buying and selling of polymer materials, providing unparalleled access to global markets and making trading easier, faster, and more profitable for all participants.
